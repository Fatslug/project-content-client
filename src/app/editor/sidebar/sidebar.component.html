Components

Hero Banner

Large background image with title/subtitle. Generally used at the top of a page.

Content Section

Section of a page dedicated to a comibination of text and images. Generally used as a paragraph/image combo.

Gallery Section

Section of a page dedicated to a gallery of images in grid or other layout.

Carousel Section

Section of a page dedicated to a carousel of images.

Contact Section

Contact section of a page to allow for an email form, or other contact details.
